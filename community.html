Meet Our Women XtraOrdinaries

Patience Igah

PATIENCE IGAH Patience Igah is a graduate of Communication Arts from the University of Uyo, with certifications in Project Management, Human Resource Management, and Leadership and management in Health from the University of Washington. She is Passionate about girl child empowerment and this has made her work with various International organizations like Family Health International, Harnessing Young People Early(HYPE Foundation), organizing and implementing projects and events to train, educate and empower people, especially the girl child. She is the Project Lead at the Roothub Accelerator Systems. She is a soft spoken, goal oriented professional with great eyes for details, resourceful and is highly positive about life. When she isn’t planning an event, you will find her designing on canva, looking up food recipes and looking for trouble…tongue out 😋.

Imelda Edet

Meet Mrs. Imelda Edet, the training lead/head of Interns at the Roothub. A position she has held since 2017. Before she joined The Roothub she was the executive assistant/social media manager at Talklogenic international limited. Imelda holds a B.A History and Diplomatic Studies from the Prestigious University of Port-Harcourt, Nigeria. She oversees the training department and impart training support systems to people. She is also responsible for creating, managing and executing projects within the cross functional systems. She is a mum and runs a clothing business. When she is not working, she becomes a monitoring spirit for the interns.

Unyime Paul

Unyime Paul is a seasoned Human Resource Manager who doubles as The Admin and Facility manager at The Roothub Accellerator Systems Limited. She has held that position since 2017. She holds a Bachelors of Business Administration(BBA) Degree in Human Resource Management from the University of Cape Coast Ghana, graduating with Honors in 2015. A friendly and boisterous young lady, working with people has been a thing for her from childhood, which perhaps pushed her to her course of study. When she's not out there being a boss and getting work done, she's having fun with her friends and family. Her mantra is “Life is not that serious make sure you live each day to the fullest”
