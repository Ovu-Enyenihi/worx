Who We Are

We are a community of women from different walks of life, blazing the trail with the use of technology and digital skills. We have seen the light and so we encourage and educate other women to embrace and use technology.

Technology is not just an industry.

How We Can Help You

Our Community

We connect you with over a thousand women from various works of life and backgrounds to help you learn, participate and grow your network.

Our Resources

We publish articles, newsletters and videos to help you start, improve, and grow your business, and your career.

Our Events/Trainings

We organize and host informative, educational, and entertaining events to help you learn, relax and connect with amazing women from various fields.

Our Driving Forces

Our Core Values

Our core values are creativity that allows our member to express themselves and diversity within our community so that women from all walks of life feel welcome and loved.

Our Vision

Our vision is to enlighten women on the need to embrace technology in their everyday lives through mentorship, coaching, training, events and showcasing women making global impact with the us of technology and ICT.

Our Mission

To grow a communtiy of women from around the world bridging the gender inequality gap in technology and using ICT tools to simplify our everyday lives
