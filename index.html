Our core values are creativity that allows our member to express themselves and diversity within our community so that women from all walks of life feel welcome and loved.

Our vision is to enlighten women on the need to embrace technology in their everyday lives through mentorship, coaching, training, events and showcasing women making global impact with the us of technology and ICT.
